Gekitai

It is a 2-player board game which is a 3-in-a-row game played on a 6x6 grid.

    Game rule:
  1. Each player have eight colored pieces and takes turn placing them anywhere on any open space on the board
  2. When placed, a piece pushes all adjacent pieces outwards one space if there is an open space for it to move to (or off the board)
  3. Pieces shoved off the board are returned to the player
The first player who either:
  1. Lines up three of their color in a row (horizontal/vertical/diagonal)
  2. Has all eight of their pieces on the board (also after pushing)
    3. is declared the winner.
Here I used 2D-array technique in Python to generate this game in practice.
